# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or any other characteristic.

We expect all community members to be respectful, professional, and constructive in all interactions.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow
- Celebrating contributions and achievements
- Asking clarifying questions when unsure
- Admitting mistakes and learning from them

### Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, appearance, race, or religion
- Sexual harassment or unwanted sexual comments or advances
- Intimidation or threats
- Insulting or derogatory comments
- Trolling, doxing, or any form of cyberbullying
- Publishing others' private information
- Unwelcome sexual attention or jokes
- Advocacy for violence or violence against any person or group
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All community interactions (GitHub, discussions, issues, PR comments)
- Social media when representing the project
- In-person meetings and events
- Private communications related to project business
- Community communication channels

This policy does not cover private conversations unless they directly impact the project community.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Direct Communication** (if safe): Address it calmly with the person involved
2. **Private Report**: Email the maintainers at [your.email@example.com]
3. **GitHub Report**: Use the "Report abuse" feature on GitHub
4. **Anonymous Report**: Fill out [anonymous form link]

**Include:**
- Description of the incident
- Involved parties (if comfortable)
- Context and when it occurred
- Any relevant links or witnesses

### Important

- All reports are taken seriously
- Confidentiality will be maintained
- Retaliation will not be tolerated
- You won't be punished for reporting in good faith

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will investigate reports and determine appropriate action, which may include:

### Level 1: Warning
- First-time violations or minor infractions
- Clear violation without intentional harm
- Private warning to cease behavior

### Level 2: Temporary Suspension
- Repeated violations despite warning
- Moderate severity offense
- 48-hour to 7-day suspension from community spaces

### Level 3: Permanent Ban
- Severe or repeated violations
- Harassment or threats
- Permanent removal from community

**Process:**
1. Investigation of the report
2. Discussion with involved parties (if appropriate)
3. Decision and notification
4. Opportunity for appeal (if requested)

## Appeals

If you believe a decision was made in error:

1. Submit a written appeal within 7 days
2. Review by impartial party
3. Final decision communicated within 14 days

## Community Responsibilities

As community members, we all have responsibility to:

- Hold ourselves and others accountable
- Speak up against violations
- Support victims without judgment
- Contribute to a safe, inclusive environment
- Give others grace and second chances (when appropriate)
- Recognize our own biases and growth areas
- Help educate and mentor others

## Maintainers' Commitment

Maintainers will:

- Actively enforce this Code of Conduct
- Be transparent about decisions
- Recuse themselves if personally involved
- Treat all reports seriously
- Protect reporter confidentiality
- Support affected community members
- Take feedback and improve the process
